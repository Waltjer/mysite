Hi there! Welcome to my personal website. I hope you’ll learn something new about me here, and maybe we can have a fun chat. I’m working hard to become a quantitative researcher in epidemiology and public health, which is a big goal of mine.

Outside of work, I’m a nerd, gamer, and pop culture enthusiast. I also enjoy technology and coding—like building this website! I love the thrill of solving problems and creating something from scratch. Once I start, I can’t stop! But deciding what to do and when to start? Well, that’s a whole other story—procrastination is a global phenomenon, after all. 😄
